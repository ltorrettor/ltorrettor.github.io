Goose Gauntlet

Goose Gauntlet is a fun turn-based word spelling game centered around teaching the players new words in an interesting and interactive way. The goal of the player is to progress through three worlds, battling enemies throughout by assembling words letter-by-letter. The final level in each world will have a challenging boss fight meant to push the playerâ€™s creativity and resourcefulness, and the game has different difficulty levels with a helpful hints system to motivate learning.

Project Background

Goose Gauntlet was created with middle school students in mind. Our motivation was to create a game that would be fun and difficult for the player without going back on what makes a game educational. The content focuses mainly on spelling and critical thinking, two skills that are often nurtured in children going through their middle school years. Using these different ideas allowed us to create something unique that we hope you will enjoy.
